- Ensure all circuits are properly insulated and protected from short circuits.
- Use resistors to prevent overcurrent through LEDs.

## Troubleshooting Guide
1. **LEDs Not Lighting Up:**
   - Check all connections and ensure correct polarity.
   - Test the power supply to ensure it meets the required voltage.
2. **Flickering LEDs:**
   - Inspect solder joints for poor connectivity.
   - Confirm that the power supply can handle the load.
