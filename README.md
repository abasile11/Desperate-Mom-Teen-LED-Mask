# Desperate Mom Teen LED Mask

## Motivation
The motivation behind the Desperate Mom Teen LED Mask project is to finally help my son regain his self confidence by eliminating his acne without having to put him on accutane.

## Goals
1. **Innovative Design**: Develop a comfortable and functional mask that integrates LED technology seamlessly.
2. **User Customization**: Allow users to customize the LED display. 
3. **Safety and Functionality**: Ensure that the mask is not functional but also of clinical strength, adhering to safety standards while easy to use.
4. **Community Engagement**: Create a community around the product where users can share design ideas, feedback, and ideas for improvement.

## Overview
The Desperate Mom Teen LED Mask is designed to merge functionality and convenience with technology. The project involves designing a user-friendly interface for customizing LED patterns so that the user can target specific areas with either red or blue light or both and ensuring that the mask is both safe to wear and at a clinical strength. In the future I would love to add a timer and app functionality.
